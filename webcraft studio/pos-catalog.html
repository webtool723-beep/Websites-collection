Interface 02 — Staff Facing

The Kitchen Dashboard
built for speed and clarity

Everything your team needs on a single screen — from incoming orders to daily summaries. No missed tickets, no confusion.

🔒

Firebase Verified-Email Auth

Only verified staff accounts can access the kitchen screen. No accidental access — your team, your data.

⚡

Real-time Orders via Supabase

New orders appear instantly using Supabase Realtime. Zero polling, zero delay — tickets land the moment they're placed.

Sound Chime on New Order

An audio alert fires the moment a new order arrives so your team never misses a ticket — even during a busy rush.

🔄

In-Stock / Out-of-Stock Toggles

Mark items 86'd in one tap. Updates propagate instantly to the customer order page.

Store Open / Close Toggle

Control when customers can place orders directly from the kitchen. Opens and closes in real-time.

✏️

Edit Order Modal

Modify items, quantities, or special instructions on any order before it's confirmed. Perfect for phone orders.
